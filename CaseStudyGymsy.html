Gymsy – an app for clients, trainers, and sports clubs

Web-App CRM IOS & Android Mobile App Fitness Scrum End To End Product Design

Gymsy is a digital ecosystem designed to structure and scale personal training within sports clubs. It connects clients, trainers, and club managers in one system - simplifying booking and engagement, streamlining trainer workflows, and giving clubs full control over offers, commissions, and performance.

🚀 Challenge

A rapidly growing network of sports clubs in Poland needed a comprehensive digital solution to streamline and scale personal training services.

The product was initially designed as a tool for freelance personal trainers, helping them manage clients, schedules, and payments independently of any sports facility.

However, during early implementation phases, it became clear that the real opportunity lay within sports clubs. As a result, the product scope shifted from supporting individual freelancers to serving entire club ecosystems.

The challenge evolved into designing one integrated solution that supports three key user groups: clients, trainers and club managers.

💢 Research & Problem Statement

I conducted market and user research to understand the key barriers faced by people who want to start personal training at the gym. The analysis covered how clients search for trainers, what clubs and trainers currently offer, and which tools are used for discovery and booking.

Key client problems:

  • No clear visibility of which trainers are available in a specific club or location
  • Limited transparency around trainers’ qualifications, reviews, and pricing
  • The need to call or message trainers to schedule sessions

I also analyzed tools used by trainers. Most rely on Instagram for promotion, which helps with visibility but not workflow management. Platforms like Booksy provide booking, reviews, and pricing features, but they are not tailored to personal training - especially when it comes to managing session packages (e.g. 10-session bundles).

Interviews with personal trainers revealed operational pain points:

  • Scheduling sessions manually via phone or chat
  • Managing calendars in personal notes or phone apps
  • No-show clients and lack of cancellation control
  • Excessive time spent on coordination instead of training delivery

💡 Key Insight & Strategic Pivot

During later stages, critical insight emerged: many trainers strongly value privacy and are reluctant to share transparent pricing or service data. This became a major adoption barrier.

As a result, targeting independent trainers proved commercially unviable.

The strategic focus therefore shifted toward developing a solution tailored to the internal needs of the growing sports club network - where transparency, structure, and operational oversight were business priorities rather than blockers.

💎 Unique Value Proposition

Gymsy is a unified ecosystem designed to create value for clients, trainers, and sports clubs - while driving business growth.

  • Clients can easily find, compare, and book training sessions with trainers while staying engaged long-term.
  • Trainers can manage sessions, communication, and settlements within a structured club system.
  • Club managers gain full control over training offers, packages, commissions, and financial performance.

For the business, Gymsy increases personal training visibility, improves conversion and package sales (easier access, promotional codes, special offers), and enables upselling of additional services - turning personal training into a scalable revenue stream.

🎮 Design, Prototyping, Testing

The app design was created in Figma using Material Design System 3. A detailed analysis of similar applications, including those for connecting service providers with service recipients (booksy, znalekarz, olx, petsy) played a significant role in the design of the application's functionality.

🎉 Release, Development & Improvement

The MVP was launched on iOS and Android in early 2025, delivering the core value proposition for clients and personal trainers. This allowed us to validate real-world usage and optimize the product before introducing the CRM layer for club managers.

Today, Gymsy supports clients, trainers, and sports clubs within one fully functional ecosystem. It is actively used by hundreds of users and dozens of personal trainers, becoming the first solution in Poland fully focused on enhancing collaboration between trainers and clients - while also providing operational control and business value for sports clubs.

Gymsy continues to evolve. In addition to the mobile app, a web version is now available for clients (no download required), currently with limited functionality that is being actively expanded.

🏆 Summary

Gymsy evolved from a freelancer-focused concept into a comprehensive ecosystem built to structure and scale personal training within sports clubs. What started as a tool for independent trainers became a strategic product supporting three interconnected user groups: clients, trainers, and club managers.

Through research-driven design and iterative development, the product addressed real market gaps - from fragmented booking processes and inefficient trainer workflows to the lack of financial transparency and operational control in clubs.

The result is a scalable, multi-platform solution (iOS, Android, Web + CRM) that improves user experience, optimizes internal operations, and transforms personal training into a measurable, growth-oriented revenue stream for sports clubs.
