CubeClub – Fitness Network Website

Webpage Fitness Club Network Design & Implementation

I independently designed and implemented a scalable website for the Cube Fitness club network, integrating it with their external customer-management system. The platform makes it easy for users to find key information about their club, promotions, class schedules, and news, while providing a modern and efficient digital experience for members.

👇 My role

  • Gathering business requirements, research and competitor analysis
  • Design and implementation
  • Maintenance and development along with the expansion of the clubs' offer and the creation of new facilities

🤝 Team

  • Me as product Designer and Developer

🚀 Challenge

In early 2024, Cube Fitness faced a digital turning point: their existing website was unable to keep up with their rapid physical expansion. I was tasked with transforming an outdated site into a high-performance, scalable platform. The objective was to build a system that supports effortless multi-location management, integrates deeply with external management software, and delivers an intuitive UX for a growing community of members.

🔍 Research

As part of the internal Cube IT team, I had a deep understanding of Cube’s business strategy. This insider perspective was crucial for aligning the UX with both technical constraints and long-term expansion goals.

Competitive Benchmarking: I audited various fitness clubs websites (from local gyms to international chains), focusing on:

  • Conversion Pathways: How top players guide users from their first visit to a successful membership purchase.
  • Membership: How customers sign up for fitness classes, how they enter the club, how they manage their membership.
  • Information Architecture: Best practices for presenting dynamic promotions and club-specific details across a growing network.

Part of my research involved a deep dive into the third-party management system used by Cube Fitness. I needed to plan how to align UX/UI solutions with the system’s existing logic to prevent development bottlenecks and ensure a stable launch.

🛠 Design & Technical Implementation

Rather than using off-the-shelf templates, I developed a high-performance website tailored to Cube Fitness’s specific needs. Using WordPress and Elementor Pro as a foundation, I was able to rapidly build a scalable architecture while maintaining full control over the UX and UI.

Core Features & Functionality:

  • Conversion-Driven Navigation: A persistent, high-visibility CTA for membership purchases and a dedicated client panel login button are always accessible.
  • Multi-Location Architecture: An intuitive club selector in the main menu that leads to dedicated subpages for each facility.
  • Comprehensive Club Pages: Each location features real-time info: addresses, opening hours, fitness schedules, promotions, honored partner cards (e.g., MultiSport), available zones/amenities, and news about the club.
  • Dynamic Content Filtering: Dedicated pages for Promotions and Trainers, both featuring smart filters to help users find relevant info for their specific club.
  • Marketing Engine: A high-impact hero carousel on the homepage highlighting the most important network-wide promotions.
  • Seamless Booking: Quick-access links to group class schedules and squash court reservations.
  • Scalable CMS: Allows to effortlessly add/edit clubs, trainers, and promotions without breaking the layout.

📈 Impact & Results

The new Cube Fitness platform has successfully transitioned from a static site to a high-traffic, conversion-driven ecosystem, supporting the brand’s rapid growth.

  • Scale & Performance: The website currently serves thousands of unique users monthly, providing a seamless bridge between digital interest and physical workouts.
  • Conversion Success: Members effortlessly navigate the purchase funnel, resulting in a consistent stream of new membership sign-ups and class bookings.
  • Data-Driven Optimization: By actively monitoring user behavior through Microsoft Clarity and Google Analytics, I can track conversion paths, identify bottlenecks, and make continuous, data-backed UX improvements.
  • Operational Efficiency: The intuitive integration with the external management system allows users to manage their accounts and schedules without friction, significantly reducing the administrative load on club staff.

🏆 Summary

This project demonstrates the power of a scalable architecture built with a business-first mindset. By taking full ownership of the design and implementation, I created a platform that is not only visually modern but also technically robust.

Key achievements:

  • Future-proof Design: System allows for the rapid addition of new clubs and promotions as the network expands.
  • Technical Synergy: Successfully aligned the UI with the technical constraints of a third-party management system to ensure a stable and reliable user experience.
  • Market Competitive: Delivered a premium digital presence that keeps Cube Fitness at the forefront of the industry, matching the standards of top-tier fitness chains.
