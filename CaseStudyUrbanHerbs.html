UrbanHerbs E-Commerce Store

E-Commerce Design & Implementation WooCommerce

I led the end-to-end design and technical implementation of the Urban Herbs e-commerce platform. For this 100% organic supplement brand, I developed a seamless, eco-conscious shopping experience by bridging the gap between strategic UX/UI design and a high-performance WooCommerce setup.

👇 My role

  • Gathering business requirements and product information, research and competitor analysis
  • Designing an online store website
  • Implementation, integration with external systems, development

🤝 Team

  • Me as Product Designer and Developer
  • One Biotechnology Specialist ensuring the substantive correctness of the presented content

🚀 Challenge

Urban Herbs are 100% organic, natural, eco-friendly food supplements. The manufacturer needed an online store that would serve an informational purpose - presenting the uniqueness and value of the product to customers and enabling them to order the product directly from the online store. In addition, the online store had to be integrated with the order processing system and marketplaces such as Allegro, Amazon, eBay, Empik.

🔍 Research

First, I had to get to know the product - all its values and unique features - to know how and what information to present to customers on the website.

Then, I analyzed the competition, compared similar companies and their supplements, and analyzed Urban Herbs' advantages over the competition.

I analyzed the target group for which the product is intended - young adults and adults who appreciate ecological values, care about their health, and can afford to spend money on premium supplements (but still at a competitive price).

At every stage of the online store's development, I consulted with a biotechnology specialist responsible for the accuracy of the content presented - what we can show and what we cannot show - these are food supplements, so they are subject to legal restrictions on sales and presentation.

💡 Conversion Strategy

To bridge the gap between digital browsing and physical quality, the store’s strategy focuses on three pillars: Trust, Transparency, and Value.

● Immediate UVP: The homepage must instantly communicate the brand’s unique value - blending urban lifestyle with scientific purity to capture attention within seconds.

● Strategic Social Proof: To address the "empty state" of a new store, we use transparent messaging that invites early adopters to build the community through first reviews.

● Radical Transparency: Product pages require a strict visual hierarchy with custom Trust Badges (Eco, Organic), deep-dive ingredient explanations, and direct access to laboratory test results.

● Reduced Friction: Conversion is maximized through high-visibility dynamic promotions, such as free shipping thresholds and seasonal bundles.

🛠 Design & Technical Implementation

Agile Design & Rapid Prototyping: I adopted an agile workflow, moving directly from Lo-Fi wireframes to a functional development environment. This approach bypassed time-consuming Hi-Fi mockups, allowing for real-time UI/UX iterations and a significantly faster time-to-market while maintaining full control over the project's direction.

Technical Implementation: The store was built using WordPress, WooCommerce, and Elementor Pro. Each screen is custom designed, without using a ready-made template.

I handled the entire end-to-end execution, from the initial setup to integrations. This included implementing Przelewy24 for payments and InPost for logistics.

Operations & Omnichannel: I integrated BaseLinker as the operational core for warehouse management, invoicing, and automated shipping. I also synchronized the store with major marketplaces like Allegro, Amazon, and eBay, creating a unified Omnichannel ecosystem.

Optimization & Launch: The final phase involved implementing technical and content refinements based on an external SEO audit. The result is a fully functional e-commerce platform that is currently live and successfully processing customer orders.

🏆 Summary

The project successfully transformed a premium supplement brand into a fully automated e-commerce engine. By combining an agile design process with a robust technical stack. The result is a scalable, omnichannel ecosystem that is currently live, processing orders.
